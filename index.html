About Us

Jyoti Aswar

"Passionate Abacus Trainer committed to unlocking the potential of every student. Dedicated to cultivating a love for learning and empowering young minds with essential mathematical skills. Inspiring a generation of confident learners through innovative teaching methods and personalized guidance."

Abacus Academy is dedicated to fostering a nurturing environment where young minds flourish. With a focus on holistic education and personalized learning, we empower students to reach their full potential. Our experienced team of educators is committed to providing innovative teaching methods, ensuring each student's success in academics and beyond. At Abacus Academy, we believe in igniting curiosity, fostering creativity, and shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
